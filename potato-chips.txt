Potato Chips

1.) Preheat Oven 375
2.) Cut Potatos With Manoline
3.) Put non stick sheet over Cooking Sheet
4.) Spread Olive Oil Over Cooking Sheet
5.) Season Potatos with Salt and Pepper
6.) Cook Potatoes for 15 Min on lower rack
7.) Flip Over Potatoes
8.) Season With Salt Pepper
9.) Cook Potatoes for 15 Min

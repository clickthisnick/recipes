Get out the following ingredients: - 5 Min


eggs
butter
bread flour
active dry yeast
salt
brown sugar (recipe says white but I don't have)

Bread Maker Steps - 1 Hr 10 Min

Put in 1 cup water
Put in 4 cups bread flour
Put in 1/4 cup brown sugar
Put in 1 tsp salt
Put in 1 1/2 tsp active dry yeast
Put in 1/4 cup butter
Put in 2 eggs
Put bread maker on dough setting (menu item 9) and hit start
Set Timer For One Hour

Clean Up - 5 Min

Put eggs back in refrigerator
Put dry active yeast back in refrigerator
Put away salt

Create Cinnamon Goodness - 35 Min

Wait for timer to go off
Mix 1/2 Cup brown sugar + 2 tsp cinnamon in bowl
Pound down dough into a flat rectangle
Spread butter + Cinnamon Sugar mixture on bread
Roll bread into loaf and put inside greased bread pan
Set timer for 30 min and let bread rise

Bake Bread - 35 Min

Wait for timer to go off
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Put bread in oven for 30 minutes

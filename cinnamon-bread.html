Get out the following ingredients: - 5 Min

measuring cups and spoons
eggs
butter
bread flour
active dry yeast
salt
brown sugar (recipe says white but I don't have)

Bread Maker Steps - 1 Hr 40 Min

Put in 1 cup water
Put in 4 cups bread flour
Put in 1/4 cup brown sugar
Put in 1 tsp salt
Put in 1 1/2 tsp active dry yeast
Put in 1/4 cup butter
Put in 2 eggs
Put bread maker on dough setting and hit start

Clean counter space:

Empty dishwasher
Load dishwasher
Put away anything not related to recipe away

Get out the following ingredients:

0.5 Red Onion and wash
3 Scallions and wash
2 Garlic Cloves
Bell Pepper and wash
2 Zuchinnis and wash
5 Crimini Mushrooms and wash
Soy Sauce
Pad Thai Sauce
Spirilizer
Vegetable Oil

Recipe

Put 3 seconds of Vegetable Oil in pantelligent on 4
Cut 0.5 Red Onion
Cut 3 Scallion
Cut Bell Pepper
Cut 2 Garlic Clove
Cut 5 Crimini Mushroom
Put vegetables in pan for 7 minutes
Spirilizer 2 Zuchinni
Rinse and put sprilizer in dishwasher
Wait for 7 min timer
Put sprilized zuchinni in the pan
Wait 2 min
Put in 1 Measuring tsp Soy Sauce
Put soy sauce back in refridgerator
Put in 2 Measuring tbsp Pad Thai Sauce
Put pad thai sauce back in refridgerator

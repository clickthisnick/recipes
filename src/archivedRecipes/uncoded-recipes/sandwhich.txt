Ingredients:
Hero
Meat - Corned Beef, Black Forest Ham - 6 Slices
Chipotle Mayonaise
Pepper Jack Cheese - 2 Slices
Lettuce - Optional - 1 Leaf

1.) Put pan to medium heat (heat 3)
2.) Butter Hero
3.) Put Hero on pan butter side down for 2 min
4.) Flip the hero, put the cheese on top for 30 seconds
5.) Take hero off pan
6.) Put meat on pan for 30 seconds
7.) Put meat on hero
8.) Put boar head Chipotle Mayonaise On sandwich
9.) Put lettuce on hero

Eat!

Crockpot
-----------------
1.) Open Frozen 2 lb Chicken Bag
2.) Put Chicken In Bowl And Fill With Hot Water
3.) Turn The Salt Grinder 15 Times
4.) Turn The Pepper Grinder 10 Times
5.) Stir With Fork
6.) Wait 25 Minutes
7.) Turn Crock Pot To High
8.) Wait 5 Minutes
9.) Put Chicken + Half Cup Of Water In Crockpot
10.) Sprinkle Seasoning In Needed
11.) Wait 2 Hours - Chicken Should Be 165 Degrees Farenheit
-----------------

Oven
-----------------
1.) Put Frozen Chicekn in bowl
2.) Fill bowl with hot water
4.) Turn The Salt Grinder 15 Times
5.) Turn The Pepper Grinder 10 Times
6.) Stir With Fork
7.) Wait 25 Minutes
8.) Turn oven to 400 degrees
9.) Put Chicken On Tin Foil in Pan
10.) Season with salt + pepper
11.) Cook for 8 minutes
12.) Flip chicken
13.) Season with salt + pepper
14.) Cook for 8 minutes
15.) Put mixture of 1 tablespoon honey, 1 tablespoon tapatio, 3 tablespoon teriyaki on chicken
16.) Broil for 3 minutes
-----------------

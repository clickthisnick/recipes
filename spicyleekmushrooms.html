Clean counter space:

Empty dishwasher
Load dishwasher
Put away anything not related to recipe away

Get out the following ingredients:

2 Leeks and wash
5 Crimini Mushrooms and wash
2 Garlic Cloves
Soy Sauce
Siracha Sauce
Pepper Flake
Hoison Sauce
Vegetable Oil

Recipe

Put 3 seconds of Vegetable Oil in pantelligent on 4
Cut in half 2 Leek
Cut leek in strips and soak in glass bowl of water
Chop 2 Garlic Clove
Cut 5 Crimini Mushroom
Put vegetables in pan for 5 minutes
Put in 1 Measuring tsp Soy Sauce
Put soy sauce back in refridgerator
Put in 0.5 Measuring tbsp Siracha Sauce
Put siracha back in refridgerator
Put in 0.25 Measuring tbsp Pepper Flake
Put pepper flake back in cupboard
Put in 1 Measuring tsp Hoison Sauce
Put hoison back in refridgerator
Wait 2 min

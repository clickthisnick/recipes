Clean counter space:

Empty dishwasher
Load dishwasher
Put away anything not related to recipe away

Get out the following ingredients:

2 Avacados
Kosher Salt
0.5 Lime
0.66 Red Onion and wash
2 Serrano Chillis and wash
Cilantro and wash
Black Pepper
0.5 Roma Tomato and wash
0.5 Scallion and wash

Recipe

Cut 2 Avacado and place in glass bowl
Put 0.75 Measuring tsp Kosher Salt in bowl
Put kosher salt back in cubboard
Mash avacado with fork
Mince 0.66 Measuring Cup Red Onion and put in bowl
Mince 2 Serrano Chilli and put in bowl
Chop 0.5 Scallion and put in bowl
Mince and deseed and depulp 0.5 Roma Tomato and put in bowl
Put 2 Measuring tbsp Cilantro in bowl
Squeeze 0.5 Lime into bowl
Put 0.25 Measuring tsp Black Pepper
Stir bowl

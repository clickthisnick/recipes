Clean counter space:

Empty dishwasher
Load dishwasher
Put away anything not related to recipe away

Get out the following ingredients:

Brown Sugar
Bread Flour
Water
Egg
Butter
Active Dry Yeast
Kosher Salt
Cinnamon

Recipe

Put in breadmaker 4 Measuring Cup Bread Flour
Put bread flour back in cuboard
Put in breadmaker 0.25 Measuring Cup Brown Sugar
Put in breadmaker 1 Measuring Cup Water
Put in breadmaker 1 Measuring tsp Kosher Salt
Put kosher salt back in cubboard
Put in breadmaker 1.5 Measuring tsp Active Dry Yeast
Put dry active yeast back in refridgerator
Put in breadmaker 0.25 Measuring Cup Butter
Put butter back on right of sink
Put in breadmaker 2 Egg
Put eggs back in refridgerator
Put bread maker on dough setting (menu item 9) and hit start
Set Timer For One Hour
Mix 0.5 Measuring Cup Brown Sugar 2 Measuring tsp Cinnamon
Put cinnamon back in cubboard
Put brown sugar back in cubboard
Wait for timer to go off
Pound down dough into a flat rectangle on floured cutting board
Spread butter + Cinnamon Sugar mixture on bread
Roll bread into loaf and put inside greased bread pan
Set timer for 30 min for bread or 25 min for cinnamon rolls
Wait for timer to go off
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Put bread in oven for 30 minutes

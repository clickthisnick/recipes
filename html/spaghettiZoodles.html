Clean counter space:

Empty dishwasher
Load dishwasher
Put away anything not related to recipe away

Get out the following ingredients:

2 Zuchinnis and wash
3 Crimini Mushrooms and wash
2 Garlic Cloves
0.25 Parmessan Cheese
Vegetable Oil
Spirilizer
Black Pepper
0.5 Spaghetti Sauce

Recipe

Put 2 seconds of Vegetable Oil in pantelligent on 4
Chop 2 Garlic Cloves
Wait 1 minute for pan to heat up
Put in 3 Crimini Mushrooms and garlic
Wait 3 minutes
Spirilizer 2 Zuchinnis
Rinse and put sprilizer in dishwasher
Put zuchinni in pan
Wait 5 minutes
Put in 0.5 Spaghetti Sauce
Turn heat to 5
Wait 5 min
Season with Black Pepper
Top with 0.25 Measuring Cup Parmessan Cheese
Put parmessan cheese back in refridgerator

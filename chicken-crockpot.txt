1.) Open Frozen 2lb Chicken Bag
2.) Run Hot Water For 10 Seconds So Its Hot
3.) Put Chicken In Bowl And Fill With Hot Water
4.) Turn The Salt Grinder 15 Times
5.) Turn The Pepper Grinder 10 Times
6.) Stir With Fork
7.) Wait 25 Minutes
8.) Turn Crock Pot To High
9.) Wait 5 Minutes
10.) Put Chicken + Half Cup Of Water In Crockpot
11.) Sprinkle Seasoning In Needed
12.) Wait 2 Hours - Chicken Should Be 165 Degrees Farenheit

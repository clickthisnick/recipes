Put all into ziplock bag:

1/2 Cup Rice

1 Tsp Parsley Or Italian Seasoning
1 Tsp Onion Powder
1/2 Overflowing Tsp Garlic Powder

1/3 Chicken Bouline Cube

------------

Cooking:

1.) Put Rice into Big Bowl
2.) Put In 9 Turns Pepper
3.) Put In 4 Turns Salt
4.) Pour Olive Oil for 5 Seconds Into Bowl
5.) Stir Rice
6.) Put In 2 Cups Water
7.) Cook for 9 Min on 8 Heat
8.) Add 1/2 Cup Water
9.) Cook for 4 Min on 6 Heat

Put all into ziplock bag:

1 Tsp Parsley
1 Tsp Onion Powder
3/4 Tsp Garlic Powder

1/2 Cup Rice

9 Turns Pepper
4 Turns Salt

1/3 Chicken Bouline Cube

------------

Cooking:

1.) Put Rice into Big Bowl
2.) Pour Olive Oil for 5 Seconds Into Bowl
3.) Stir Rice
4.) Put In 2 Cups Water
5.) Cook for 9 Min on 8 Heat
6.) Add 1/2 Cup Water
7.) Cook for 4 Min on 6 Heat
